In previous incarnations, I’ve helped Sandbag poke the beast of the EU Emissions Trading System and put 200 years worth of parliamentary speeches online. In general, you can find my code on github, and my photos on flickr and instagram. You can contact me at louise dot crow at gmail dot com.
